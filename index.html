What is Science on Wheels?

Science on Wheels is a mobile science van initiative by Plaksha University, IIT Mandi, and IDYM Foundation designed to bring hands-on STEM learning to PM Shri schools across Punjab. Through interactive exhibits, robotics demonstrations, space technology education, and water testing, the program aims to spark curiosity and bridge the exposure gap in rural, border, and remote areas.
