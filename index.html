Our Mission

Reaching these children through mobile science labs can bridge the exposure gap, ignite curiosity, and build foundational STEM interest in the communities that need it most. Over two years the van will spend four months in each state, engaging 45–50 schools per state per year in coordination with district administrations and Block Education Officers.

Four Modules on Wheels

Every school visit brings science exhibits and activities across four educational components.

Where We Go

Districts were identified in consultation with administrative authorities, prioritising rural, border, remote and high SC/ST-population areas.

◆ Base hubs · ● Target districts. Schools visited will appear on this map as the mission progresses.

How a Mission Works

Phase 1 · Planning (1 month per state)

Development of exhibits, models, tutorials and engaging content. Route and path planning across districts and blocks, school selection with BEOs, and coordination with speakers, experts and scientists.

Phase 2 · Execution (3 months per state)

One school per day, ~15 days a month. Demos also run in public places, open grounds and child hostels, plus technical events and student fests for wider community impact.

Maintenance & Impact

After each state tour the van returns to its base (IDYM Antariksh Prayogshala in Dumar, Chhattisgarh / Plaksha / IIT Mandi) for exhibit repair and upgrades. Field assistants collect feedback and run impact analysis in every state.
